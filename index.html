RAVENS

Key Moments in History

In 1996, the Cleveland Browns relocated to Baltimore, and the Ravens were born, named after Edgar Allan Poe’s famous poem "The Raven."

In 2000, under coach Brian Billick, the Ravens won their first Super Bowl, defeating the New York Giants 34-7.

In 2012, the Ravens won their second Super Bowl, defeating the San Francisco 49ers 34-31, led by MVP Joe Flacco.
